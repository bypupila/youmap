# Travel Web

Glossary for sponsor styling and preview behavior in the creator panel.

## Language

**Sponsor style**:
The visual variant assigned to a sponsor card.
_Avoid_: template, design, diseño

**CTA rojo**:
The sponsor style used for a direct action message on a sponsor card.
_Avoid_: red button, red banner

**Sponsor preview**:
The live rendering of the selected sponsor style on a selected video card.
_Avoid_: mockup, sample card

**Sponsor banner**:
The sponsor area inside a sponsor card whose colors can reflect the sponsor brand.
_Avoid_: ad banner, custom card

**Pausar sponsor**:
The reversible action that deactivates a sponsor while preserving its history and metrics.
_Avoid_: eliminar sponsor, borrar sponsor

**Video de preview**:
One of the selected videos used to validate how the sponsor looks before saving.
_Avoid_: test video

**Multi sponsor**:
The combined sponsor variant used when two or more sponsors apply to the same card.
_Avoid_: multi bar, mixed sponsors

**Panel del creador**:
The private operational workspace where a travel creator manages their public map, videos, polls, sponsors, audience signals, and priority actions.
_Avoid_: admin global, superadmin panel, creator-panel legacy

**Metricas del mapa**:
First-party audience and interaction metrics captured inside Travel Your Map, separate from YouTube metrics.
_Avoid_: YouTube Analytics, YouTube Console data

**Accion prioritaria**:
The single recommended operational action surfaced to the creator based on map health, pending review, audience activity, monetization opportunity, or sync state.
_Avoid_: alerta generica, tip, sugerencia

**Export de reporte**:
The brand-ready printable version of a sponsor report that can be saved as PDF from the browser.
_Avoid_: PDF automatico, server-side PDF, captura manual

**Renovacion comercial**:
A new campaign proposal created from a delivered or paid campaign to continue a brand partnership.
_Avoid_: duplicar campaña, reabrir campaña, extender sin propuesta

**Acceso de marca**:
The email-and-code gate that protects a private brand portal without turning the brand contact into a platform user.
_Avoid_: usuario brand, viewer de marca, login global de marca

**Business OS readiness**:
The private operational status that tells the creator whether the business surfaces have the required platform capabilities and optional email configuration available.
_Avoid_: debug de migraciones, healthcheck generico

**Agenda comercial**:
The creator's prioritized operational view of upcoming or overdue brand deliverables and payments.
_Avoid_: calendario generico, lista de tareas manual

**Export comercial**:
The creator-owned CSV export and shareable summary of leads, campaigns, deliverables, payments, and pipeline.
_Avoid_: backup tecnico, reporte de sponsor, analytics publico

**Colaboracion**:
A commercial campaign subtype inside Negocio used to evaluate, accept, track, and close brand exchanges such as paid sponsors, barter, hotels, experiences, products, and affiliate deals.
_Avoid_: sponsor publico automatico, tabla separada de campanas

**Balance de colaboracion**:
The creator-private estimate and final record of cash, in-kind value, own costs, and effort for a collaboration.
_Avoid_: contabilidad fiscal, reporte publico de marca, rate card

**Sponsor Kit**:
A private, tokenized commercial presentation shared with potential sponsors, separate from the public viewer profile and public Media Kit.
_Avoid_: perfil publico viewer, reporte financiero interno
